Who are we?

We’re a group of teens making a difference. We’re open to any high school student in Fayette County (public and private schools) who wants to be a peer leader and have an impact on the community.

What do we do?

We educate youth and adults on the risks involved in underage drinking through presentations to both adults and teens, television appearances, radio spots, and special projects.

When do we meet?

We meet approximately once a month for pizza and discussion. We currently meet on Sunday afternoons. Times are decided by the group, as we want to work around everyone’s busy schedule.

Why get involved?

Show your peers that there are betterthings to do than drink alcohol. Also, this coalition is a unique leadership opportunity to interact with community leaders concerning something that truly makes a difference in people’s lives.

How are we connected to the community?

This youth-led organization works with local and state leaders. We’re sponsored by the Fayette County ASAP. The Youth Coalition serves as the Youth Voice for this county-wide group. Teens have the opportunity to interact with community leaders & advise them on how to design programs to reduce teen substance abuse.
